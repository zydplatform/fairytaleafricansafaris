Trip Overview

FairyTaleAfrican Safaris unleashes Uganda’s most popular safari combining wildlife, Chimpanzee and Gorilla adventure in just 10 days. Visit Uganda’s top beautiful parks and witness the best Uganda tourist circuit enabling you encounter the rarest of the rare in the Africa jungles including The Elephant, Lion, Cape buffalo, Rothschild’s giraffe, hartebeest, Waterbuck, Oribi, Bushbuck,  Bohor reedbuck, hyena among other not forgetting the greatest primates including the Chimpanzees and the Gorillas

Detailed itinerary

Day by day trip details
Expand All
Day 1 : Kampala- Murchison Falls
Depart early morning to Murchison Falls National Park -north-western Uganda through the famous Luwero Triangle and, travel to Masindi via Ziwa Rhino Project. A visit to the Ziwa Rhino Sanctuary for Rhino trekking is a must. Later proceed to Murchison Falls .

Overnight: Paraa Safari Lodge (Luxury accommodation) | Red Chili (Budget Accommodation) -FB

Day 2 : Game Drive and Boat Cruise on the Nile
After an early morning cup of African Coffee, you will go for a morning game drive. Here you will have a chance of spotting Elephants, Lions, Cape buffaloes, Rothschild’s giraffes, hartebeest, Waterbucks, Oribi, Bushbuck, Bohor reedbuck, hyenas and a leopard if you are lucky. Return to the lodge for breakfast and lunch. Afternoon boat cruise upstream the calm Victoria Nile to the bottom of the falls stopping just tens of meters from the “Devil’s Cauldron”. En-route, marvel at the toothsome hippos, crocodiles and over this 17km stretch you may see elephants, waterbucks, Uganda Kobs and crocodiles. This is also a zone of many bird species like Goliath heron, Egyptian goose, pelican bee-eater, kingfisher, hornbill, cormorant and the rare shoebill stork. There you leave the boat and start your – quite heavy – hike to the Top of the Falls (approx. 1 hour). See where the world’s longest river explodes violently through a narrow cleft in the Rift Valley escarpment to plunge into a frothing pool 43m below.

Overnight: Paraa Safari Lodge (Luxury accommodation) | Red Chili (Budget accommodation) - FB

Day 3 : Murchison Falls-Kibale Forest National Park
Depart for Kibale Forest National Park driving to the South Western part of Uganda Fort Portal. You will traverse through verdant countryside and past traditional homesteads and farms along Albertine Rift escarpments. Fort-Portal rests in the shadow of the fabled Mountains of the Moon.

Overnight: Kyaninga Lodge (Luxury accommodation) | Chimps Nest (Budget Accommodation) - FB

Day 4 : Chimpanzee Trekking & Bigodi Walk
Morning Chimpanzee tracking. Start with a briefing at Kanyanchu Tourist Centre where tourist activities begin and set out for your acitivity. Chances of viewing the chimps are over 90% but not a guarantee. Kibale National Park is one of the beautiful and stunning forests in the Uganda. It is home to our closest living relatives the endangered Chimpanzees, the threatened Red colobus monkey and the rare L’Hoest’s monkey. The forest also boasts of having one of the highest diversity and density of primates in Africa totaling 13 species including the Black and white colobus, Blue monkey, Grey-cheeked mangabey, Red tailed monkey and Bush babies and Pottos. Return to the lodge for lunch and later visit Bigodi Wetland Sanctuary from where you will take on a guided walk around the Swamp which will provide you with the opportunity to see a wide range of flora and fauna. View a variety of butterflies, birds and other insects. Have a tour of the crater region around Ndali lodge.

Overnight: Kyaninga Lodge (Luxury Accommodation) | Chimps Nest (Budget Accommodation) - FB

Day 5 : Kibale -Queen Elizabeth NP | Boat Trip
After breakfast, drive to Queen Elizabeth National Park viewing the mystical Rwenzori Mountain range for much of the journey. Check in at your accommodation facility as you wait for your lunch. Afternoon Launch cruise along Kazinga Channel – one that joins Lakes Edward and George. The cruise is a wonderful way to see hippo, buffalo, kob and the occasional Nile crocodile (which up until recently did not inhabit this area) and a spectacular bird watching on the water bank.

Overnight: Kyambura Gorge Lodge (Luxury accommodation) | Buffalo Safari Lodge (Budget accommodation) - FB

Day 6 : Game Drive & Nature Walk
Rise very early with your packed lunch take on a morning game drive to catch-up with the early risers and predators returning to their hideouts. You will most likely meet grazing Hippos, Elephants, and Lions, spotted Hyenas, bush buck, water buck, stripped jackal, comical warthogs and the elusive leopard. Proceed to Maramagambo forest for a nature walk. Many surprises await you here for example pythons are often observed in the crevices of the bat cave floor using the bats as a source of food. The cave is near a picturesque formerly Blue lake and Hunters cave. You may also see forest birds such as bat hawks, brown eared woodpecker and other primates. Return for dinner and overnight stay.

Overnight: Kyambura Gorge Lodge (Luxury accommodation) | Buffalo Safari Lodge - FB

Day 7 : Queen Elizabeth NP to Bwindi Impenetrable National Park
With previous day’s memories, enjoy your breakfast ready for a +/- 5hrs drive to Bwindi Impenetrable Forest National Park. You will drive along the deep and wide dry craters that are occupied by savanna grasses and acacia. The drive takes you through the unique western rift valley escarpment and the (southern part of Queen Elizabeth National Park) Ishasha sector where you will take on yet another game drive in search of tree climbing lions before proceeding to Bwindi Impenetrable National Park.

Overnight: Buhoma Lodge (Luxury accommodation) | Buhoma Community Banda (Budget accommodation) - FB

Day 8 : Full Day Gorilla Tracking
Depart the Lodge with a packed lunch as you go for a full day Gorilla Tracking. Many who take part in this activity consider it the high light of their entire African safari. The thrill of your encounter will erase the difficulties of the potentially strenuous trek. Bring jungle boots and rain jackets because the park is often wet. Spend time in awe watching the gorillas go about their activities. Each gorilla safari encounter is different and has its own rewards, but you are likely to enjoy the close view of adults feeding, grooming and resting as the youngsters frolic and swing from vines in a delightfully playful display.

Overnight: Buhoma Lodge (Luxury accommodation) | Buhoma Community Banda (Budget accommodation) - FB

Day 9 : Bwindi -Lake Mburo National Park
Check out of your hotel and embark on a journey to Lake Mburo National +/- 4hrs. Make a stopover at Igongo Cultural Cntre to explore and learn the Bahima and Ankole culture. Arrive at the lodge in time for an evening game drive. Lake Mburo National park is renowned for zebras, impalas, buffaloes, hippos, topi and elands including the rare shoebill, crested crane, pelicans, fish eagle, heron and many water birds. Evening boat cruise amidst the Hippos is amazing.

Overnight: Mihingo Lodge (Luxury Accommodation) |Arcadia Cottages (Budget Accommodation) – FB.

Day 10 : Mburo-Entebbe Departure
Have a early morning breakfast and then go for a morning game drive. Continue your drive with a stopover at the Equator crossing with your lunch enroute before you drive to back to Entebbe to catch up your international flight. ******End of your safari and our services
