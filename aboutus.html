Terms & Privacy

To cover our estimated loss caused by the and as we may be unable to resell your holiday, cancellation charges excluding insurance will be levied per person

a) For any safari cancellations made 60 days prior to your date of departure, a full refund of the initial deposit fee will be made

b) If the safari/tour is cancelled between 60-45 days prior to your departure date, a 50% refund of the initial deposit will be made. For any cancellations made less than 45 days before departure date there will be no refund

c) In commencement of a safari no refunds will be made, as no any refund will be available in respect to any safaris accommodation, car services, or any inclusions not utilized.

d) For gorilla tracking activity, the company doesn’t hold any liability for any circumstances that might lead to cancellation of the activity. Refunds are always at the discretion of the relevant authorities in each country and we cannot hold liability of them, however the company will endeavor to obtain the refund later be sent to the soonest of time.

Note; In case of any cancellations by you or any member of you (group safaris), you are required to advise us with a written notification as soon. Any cancellation will not be void/ deemed valid until receipt of a written notification.
