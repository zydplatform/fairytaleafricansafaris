Trip Overview

The Great Migration. Almost two million animals move across the Serengeti and Mara plains in search of food and water. The herds move from the southern plains of the Serengeti, through the western section into the Masai Mara in Kenya where they roam for a few months before heading south into the Serengeti again. With over half of the world’s remaining mountain gorilla population, it’s no surprise that the majority of guests visit Rwanda to track and view our highly endangered cousins. Uganda and Rwanda are superb for primates in general, including the fun-loving Sykes monkey, the Golden Monkey and the boisterous chimpanzee in the Nyungwe Forest and Kibale forest

Day 1 : Arrive at Kilimanjaro Airport in Kilimanjaro

On arrival at the Kilimanjaro International Airport, you will be met by our representative who will transfer you to Arusha for check-in and dinner at the hotel.


Overnight: Four Points By Sheraton, Arusha Hotel.

Day 2 : Transfer from Arusha – Lake Manyara – Ngorongoro Nat. Conservation Area

After your breakfast, you will depart with picnic lunch boxes for a full-day tour of the Lake Manyara National Park.

Tucked below the majesty of the Rift Valley wall, Lake Manyara National Park consists of a thin green band of forest, flanked by the sheer 600 m high red and brown cliffs of the escarpment on one side and by the white-hot shores of an ancient soda lake on the other. Acacia woodland shelters the park’s famous tree-climbing lions, lying languidly among the branches in the heat of the day. Lake Manyara safaris offer many unique views including elephant, giraffe, buffalo, and wildebeest can be found grazing in unexpected clearings or heading towards the water to drink or wash, and the rivers and riverbeds provide scenic vistas for possible animal spotting. Warthogs seem to thrive here, growing notably fat and tusked, and it is a natural playground for baboons and monkeys.

Later on, in the afternoon, you will continue with the drive into the Ngorongoro National Conservation Area, where you will arrive in time for check-in at your camp which is located at the crater rim.


Overnight: Serena Ngorongoro Lodge

Day 3 : Full Day at Ngorongoro Crater Area

After your breakfast, you will depart for a whole day crater tour with picnic lunch boxes that you will take on the crater floor. Thereafter, you will have an afternoon game drive in the Ngorongoro Crater and later return to your camp.

This area’s main attraction is the Ngorongoro Crater, fondly known as “Africa’s Garden of Eden”. A candidate for the new Seven Wonders of the World, this massive crater holds its own in that category. Being the world’s largest unbroken caldera, Ngorongoro is home to over 25,000 animals. It is considered one of the best game viewing spots in the world with a chance to see the big five, zebras and wildebeests. The crater holds almost every individual species of wildlife in East Africa including the extremely rare and critically endangered Black Rhino


Overnight: Serena Ngorongoro Lodge

Day 4 : Ngorongoro Nat. Conservation Area – Serengeti Nat. Park (Central)

After your breakfast, you will depart for the Serengeti National Park where you will arrive in time for check-in and lunch at the camp. You will then spend the afternoon at leisure before going for an evening game drive until dusk when you return to your camp.

Serengeti is the stage on which the most spectacular wonders of nature takes place, the Great Migration. It is on this park that millions of ungulates comprising of wildebeests and zebras make that all-important trek from the Serengeti to the Masai Mara National Park in Kenya. But that’s not all the Serengeti has to offer, it boasts the largest and highest concentration of wildlife in the world. Spanning an impressive 15,000 sq. km area, this savannah is home to all the members of the Big 5, the splendid cheetah, gazelles, African wild dog, giraffes and approximately 500 species of bird, making it a bird watchers paradise as well.


Overnight: Ole Serai Luxury Tented Camp – Seronera (Central Serengeti)

Day 6 : Another Full Day Serengeti Nat. Park (North)

After your breakfast, you will move northwards to the Kogatende area of Serengeti where you will arrive in time for check-in and lunch at the Camp. You then spend the afternoon at leisure before going for an evening game drive until dusk when you return to your camp.

Overnight: Ole Serai Luxury Tented Camp - Kogatende (Northern Serengeti)

Day 5 : Full Day at Serengeti Nat. Park (Central)

Today you will spend the whole day in the central Serengeti area with both the morning and evening game drives.

Overnight: Ole Serai Luxury Tented Camp – Seronera (Central Serengeti)

Day 7 : Serengeti Nat. Park (North)

Today you will spend the whole day in the northern Serengeti area with both the morning and evening game drives.

Overnight: Ole Serai Luxury Tented Camp - Kogatende (Northern Serengeti)

Day 8 : Transfer from Serengeti Nat. Park (North) to Masai Mara National Game Reserve

In the morning, have an en-route game drive where you will be connecting from your lodge to the airstrip to catch your flight to the Maasai Mara and then go through the border immigration cross-checks and procedures and after you get on your flight heading to Migori airstrip and then proceed to your camp for relaxation, depending on the arrival time you will have an evening game drive and around the Mara and back to the camp to prepare for tomorrows activities.

Overnight: Entim Mara Camp

Day 9 : Full Day Masai Mara Game reserve

You will have two more days of wildlife-viewing opportunities in the Masai Mara Reserve, also known as the “Jewel of Africa,” a site with one of the largest and the most spectacular animal migrations in the world. The reserve is inhabited by many of Africa's most popular wild animals, including lion, cheetah, elephant, leopard, black rhino and hippo. There are also over 500 resident species of birds in the park including ostrich, larks, and sunbirds as well as the lovely Lilac-breasted Roller.

Overnight: Entim Mara Camp

Day 10 : Have a morning game drive & Evening Cultural Encounter with Maasai Warriors

You will wake up in the morning and then have breakfast later transfer for your days’ game viewing activities, this park is so large and you will still be able to find more unseen wildlife and with luck go the Mara triangle and find more cats 

Overnight: Entim Mara Camp

Day 11 : Transfer from Masai Mara to Nairobi connecting to Kigali Airport.

Have breakfast and check out from Entim to the airstrip where you will board your flight from Maasai Mara to Wilson airport and then have lunch, later you are transferred to the Jomo Kenyatta International Airport in time for your evening departure flight to Kigali and your other GoExplore Expert will pick you up from the airport to your hotel in Kigali.

Overnight: Kigali Marriot Hotel

Day 12 : Transfer by Road to Volcanoes National park

In the morning you will have breakfast and at leisure and then your guide will transfer you from Kigali heading to Musanze to the volcanoes national park where you will have lunch at lodge in Volcanoes afternoon at leisure and get prepared for your tomorrow’s activities

Overnight: Ingagi Park View Lodge

Day 13 : Gorilla Tracking Day

After breakfast, we head for Volcanoes National Park headquarters again, where the briefing on gorilla trekking begins at 7H00. At 08H00 it starts. They are assigned on-site to 8 different gorilla families, which are visited by different trekking tours (classification on the spot according to fitness degree). The Volcanoes National Park protects the steep slopes of this magnificent mountain range and is home to the rare mountain gorilla. After you have found the gorillas, you have an hour with the remarkably peaceful gorillas. and in the evening, we visit the Gorilla Guardians village where you will learn the way of life in Rwanda and then back to the lodge.

Overnight: Ingagi Park View Lodge

Day 14 : Transfer back to Kigali in the afternoon for your flight back home

Have breakfast and then later your GoExplore Expert will transfer you from the lodge and head to Kigali where you will go on for a visit to the genocide museum, this will be for like -/+4hours depending on your flight details have lunch and then your GoExplore guide will transfer you to the airport
