Trip Overview

Drown in the highlights of Rwanda and Tanzania tracking the gorillas and big game of East Africa. This Remarkable 12-day safari explores the wildlife-rich Ngorongoro Crater and famous Northern Serengeti, before flying to Rwanda to track the magnificent mountain gorillas. Staying in fantastic accommodation throughout, and offering some of Africa's best wildlife experiences, this is a wonderful safari for those who want to see the best bits. This highlights safari can also be done in reverse, starting in Rwanda, giving you the option of a beach extension to the stunning Tanzanian Coast or 'spice island' of Zanzibar for a bit of relaxation after your adventure.

Day by day trip details

Day 1 : Arrive at Kigali International Airport

On Arrival At Kigali Airport where you will go through the immigration checks and COVID-19 Checks, then taken to you isolation for 48hours or presenting a 72-hour current COVID-19 Certificate then we shall pick you up from your center of Quarantine a transfer you from There and we proceed to head to Volcanoes National Park.

Overnight: Heaven Retreat Boutique Hotel

Day 2 : Under Quarantine

Spend another Day Under Quarantine and relaxing waiting for your Results to come out.

Overnight: Heaven Retreat Boutique Hotel

Day 3 : Transfer to Volcanoes National Park

Have Breakfast in the morning and then you will meet and greet by Your GoExplore Safaris Guide who will take you through the briefing and guide you on the flow of the trip, however we shall make a stop at Genocide Museum and then later head out to Volcanoes Arriving in the afternoon in time for lunch at the lodge 

Overnight: One & Only Gorillas Nest

Day 4 : Full Day Gorilla Tracking Day

After breakfast, you will report to the park headquarters in Kinigi where your guide will accomplish the necessary formalities for you of allocating you a Gorilla family to track. You will meet your ranger guide(s) for a briefing about golden monkey tracking formality and how everything will be conducted from the start to the finish point; thereafter start your golden monkey tracking experience. You may also encounter different bird species as you track the Gorilla. Return afterward for lunch at your hotel and if time allows, visit the Ibiwagu traditional village, where you will have a cultural experience with the local community who display their traditional ways of living

Overnight: One & Only Gorillas Nest

Day 5 : Transfer Back to Kigali For Flight to Arusha.

Have Breakfast, and then you will head out from the Lodge to Musanze Airstrip for your flight to Kigali for -/+45 minutes arriving mid-morning and then transfer to Serengeti National park arriving in the afternoon, you other GoExplore Guide will be waiting for you and pick you up then begin your afternoon game viewing as you head to the camp for lunch and then short leisure time then have an evening walk around the camp with a guided ranger.

Overnight: &Beyond Serengeti Under Canvas Camp

Day 6 : Full Day Game Viewing

Morning at Breakfast and then you transfer for your game drive, with picnic lunch and then you proceed with your game drive in the afternoon and back to the camp in the evening.

Overnight: &Beyond Serengeti Under Canvas Camp

Day 7 : Another Full Day Game Viewing & Morning Got Air Balloon

Have coffee and then transfer to the starting point for the Hot Airballon, today morning you will have game viewing through the sergeant skies and then afternoon drive around the different section of the park

Overnight: &Beyond Serengeti Under Canvas Camp

Day 8 : Transfer to Ngorongoro Crater Area

As you transfer from Serengeti, this will be by road so that you can go experiencing various wildlife along the way arriving in the afternoon for lunch and then in the have a walk along the crater rim with one of the guides at the lodge and Ranger.

Overnight: &Beyond Ngorongoro Crater Lodge

Day 9 : Full Day Game Viewing and Descending into the Game

Morning at Breakfast and then you transfer for your game drive, with picnic lunch and then you proceed with your game drive in the afternoon and back to the camp in. the evening

Overnight: &Beyond Ngorongoro Crater Lodge

Day 10 : Transfer to The Airstrip in Karatu

Have Breakfast and you will get on the small caravan plane heading to Tarangire national park in the down of the section arriving in the afternoon and have a game viewing safari in the evening.

Overnight: Oliver’s Camp

Day 11 : Another Full Day Game Viewing

Morning at Breakfast and then you transfer for your game drive, with picnic lunch and then you proceed with your game drive in the afternoon and back to the camp in. the evening

Overnight: Oliver’s Camp

Day 12 : Transfer by Flight to Arusha

Have breakfast and as you proceed with your journey have an Enroute drive towards the Kuro Airstrip. And then you will catch your flight heading to Arusha for your flight Back Home.
