Trip Overview

This trip will take you to Bwindi Forest - Home of Mountain Gorillas, where the borders of Rwanda, the Democratic Republic of Congo, and Uganda meet. This Gorilla discovery trip is a trip not soon to be forgotten. You will meet the rare and endangered mountain Gorillas.

Detailed itinerary

Day by day trip details
Expand All
Day 1 : Arrival at Kigali

Today you will arrive at Kigali international Airport. Upon arrival, you will be met by a GoExplore representative who will welcome you and transfer you to your accommodation for an overnight. 

Overnight: Heaven Boutique Hotel

Day 2 : Drive to Bwinidi Impenetrable National Park

After breakfast, you will drive to Bwindi impenetrable Forest National Park crossing the border of Rwanda and Uganda. The Forest is home to the endangered Mountain Gorillas usually refered to as Gentle Giants. Once you have settled in, you will have a boat ride on Lake Mulehe or visit the Batwa community of you go for the high end option. You will have enough rest as you prepare for the next day’s activity.

Overnight: Lake Mulehe Safari Lodge

Day 3 : Gorilla trekking

After breakfast with your packed lunch, your guide will drive you to the park headquarters for briefing about the do’s and don’ts during your gorilla trekking. Your gorilla family tracking will start immediately after the briefing. This is a memorable and an amazing experience watching the Mountain gorillas feed, play around and also do their day to day activities. This activity can take 2 hours or even up to 8 hours since its dependent on the location and movement of the gorillas. Also expect to walk long distances in muddy and steep conditions- this will require you to have a walking stick. On meeting the gorillas, you will spend one hour with them as you take photos. Later, trek back to the park headquarters to pick your certificates and return back to the lodge.  

Overnight: Lake Mulehe Safari Lodge

Day 4 : Drive back to Kigali and Depart

After breakfast, you will be transferred to back to Kigali. Upon arrival and time allowing, you will go for a city tour of Kigali where you will visit the Genocide museum. Thereafter proceed to the airport for your departure flight. 


Urakoze! Thank you for choosing GoExplore Safaris to be your African Guide and we hope to take you on more East African Journeys.
